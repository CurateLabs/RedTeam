# Lineage & source

RedTeam's techniques trace primarily to the **UFMCS *Red Team Handbook* v9.0** (TRADOC G-2, University of Foreign Military and Cultural Studies) — a U.S. Government work (17 U.S.C. § 105).

## History (brief)

UFMCS taught red teaming as **applied critical thinking** and **groupthink mitigation** for organizational decision-making — not cybersecurity. The handbook unified facilitation tools, assumption checks, and cultural analysis into one curriculum.

RedTeam is an **independent civilian adaptation**. We are not affiliated with the Army, TRADOC, or UFMCS.
