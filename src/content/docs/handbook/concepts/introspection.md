**Chapter:** [Chapter 2 — Self-Awareness & Reflection](/RedTeam/handbook/chapters/self-awareness-reflection/)

## Summary

Deliberate inward examination — journaling, reflection time, structured exercises.

## When to use

Daily practice for facilitators; before high-stakes reviews.

## Method

Short structured reflection: what did I assume? what did I avoid saying? what would change my mind?

## RedTeam notes

Lightweight reflection prompts replace mandatory military journaling.
