## Summary

Structured response pattern ensuring respectful listening and ordered contribution.

## When to use

When interruptions and talking-over destroy psychological safety.

## Method

Speaker holds floor; next speaker responds to prior only; facilitator enforces order.

## RedTeam notes

Category: **facilitation**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
