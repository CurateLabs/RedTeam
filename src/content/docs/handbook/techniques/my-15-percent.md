## Summary

Each person owns the slice of the problem they can influence.

## When to use

Diffusion of responsibility; "not my job" in group failures.

## Method

Each answers: what 15% can I own? Aggregate ownership map.

## RedTeam notes

Category: **divergent**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
