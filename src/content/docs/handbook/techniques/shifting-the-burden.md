## Summary

Push past the obvious fix to ask who or what structurally bears the load.

## When to use

Symptom fixes that recur; blame on individuals.

## Method

Ask: what system structure makes this outcome rational? Who actually carries the burden?

## RedTeam notes

Category: **strategic**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
