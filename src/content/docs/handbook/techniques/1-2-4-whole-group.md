**Command:** `/redteam groupthink`

## Summary

Individual → pairs → fours → whole group; staged inclusion of every voice.

## When to use

30–60 min; need all voices before groupthink sets in.

## Method

One: silent write. Two: pairs. Four: combine pairs. Whole: plenary synthesis.

## RedTeam notes

Category: **facilitation**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
