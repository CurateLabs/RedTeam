The technique catalog for Handbook v10. Most tools support **ACT**, **GTM**, or **both**. Tools are meant to **sequence**, not stand alone.

Browse by category below. Each item has its own page in [`techniques/`](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/) or [`concepts/`](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/).


### Facilitation

- [ACT–GTM Matrix](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/act-gtm-matrix/) — Table matching tools to ACT, GTM, or both — pick and sequence by situation.
- [Ideal Group Process](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/ideal-group-process/) — Diverge → analyze → debate → converge loop with rotating ACT/GTM tools.
- [1-2-4-Whole Group](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/1-2-4-whole-group/) — Individual → pairs → fours → whole group; staged inclusion of every voice.
- [1 on 1, 2 on 2, Exchange Emissaries](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/1-on-1-2-on-2-exchange-emissaries/) — Subgroup dialog with emissaries cross-pollinating between groups.
- [Circle of Voices](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/circle-of-voices/) — Round-robin: each speaks once before anyone speaks twice.
- [Circular Response](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/circular-response/) — Structured response pattern ensuring respectful listening and ordered contribution.
- [Fishbowl](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/fishbowl/) — Inner group discusses, outer observes; swap roles to surface dynamics.
- [Think-Write-Share](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/think-write-share/) — Silent individual write before any discussion — defeats anchoring.
- [Troika Consulting](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/troika-consulting/) — Client presents; three consultants discuss while client listens.
- [Gallery Walk](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/gallery-walk/) — Post ideas; participants silently annotate and build on others' work.
- [Dot Voting](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/dot-voting/) — Allocate limited votes across options for quick prioritization.
- [Sequence / Playbooks](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/sequence/) — Chain `/redteam` commands for a decision type and the time available.

### Divergent

- [Brainstorming](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/brainstorming/) — Volume-first idea generation with deferred judgment.
- [5 Will Get You 25](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/5-will-get-you-25/) — Each person generates five ideas; combine for quantity before quality.
- [Yes, And…](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/yes-and/) — Build on prior ideas without blocking — improv rule for ideation phases.
- [Outside-In Thinking](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/outside-in-thinking/) — Import solutions or frames from unrelated domains.
- [Mind Mapping](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/mind-mapping/) — Branching visual map from a central concept.
- [String of Pearls](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/string-of-pearls/) — Sequential "what if" chain along a narrative thread.
- [What If? Analysis](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/what-if-analysis/) — Explore consequences when a key variable changes.
- [My 15%](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/my-15-percent/) — Each person owns the slice of the problem they can influence.
- [Appreciative Interview](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/appreciative-interview/) — Explore what works before fixing what doesn't — expands solution space.

### Analytical

- [5 Whys](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/5-whys/) — Iterative why-questions to reach root causes and hidden assumptions.
- [Analysis of Competing Hypotheses (ACH)](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/analysis-of-competing-hypotheses/) — Matrix scoring evidence against mutually exclusive hypotheses.
- [Key Assumptions Check](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/key-assumptions-check/) — List what must be true for the plan to work; flag critical unvalidated beliefs.
- [Assumption Sensitivity Analysis](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/assumption-sensitivity-analysis/) — Rank assumptions by what happens if each is wrong (collapse vs. absorb).
- [Argument Deconstruction](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/argument-deconstruction/) — Separate premises, inference, and conclusion; test each link.
- [Frame Audit](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/frame-audit/) — Examine the lens defining the problem — scope, metrics, causality, values.
- [4 Ways of Seeing](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/4-ways-of-seeing/) — Reframe as problem, polarity, pattern, or opportunity.
- [Problem Restatement](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/problem-restatement/) — Rephrase the problem multiple ways before solving.
- [6 Words](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/6-words/) — Distill the issue to six words — forces clarity.
- [SEE-I](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/see-i/) — State, Elaborate, Exemplify, Illustrate — clarify before debate.
- [Analogy Suitability Analysis](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/analogy-suitability-analysis/) — Test whether the analogy in use actually fits.
- [Logic of Failure](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/logic-of-failure/) — How can well-intentioned system logic still produce failure?
- [Critical Variables (CVs)](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/critical-variables/) — Dynamic factors that drive outcomes — often mapped to an Operating Landscape Map.
- [Who Else?](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/who-else/) — Whose perspective is missing from this analysis?
- [Who Am I?](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/who-am-i/) — Introspective identity/bias surfacing before engaging others.
- [Outside View / Reference Class Forecasting](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/outside-view/) — Use base rates from similar past attempts to test an inside-view forecast.
- [Incentive & Goodhart Analysis](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/incentives/) — Examine how metrics and incentives may be gamed or create perverse second-order effects.
- [Ladder of Inference](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/ladder/) — Separate observable data from interpretation and conclusion.
- [Calibration](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/calibrate/) — Grade claims as fact, inference, or guess; attach confidence and disconfirmers.
- [RFC Review](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/rfc/) — Review engineering decisions for options, reversibility, and operational consequences.
- [Cognitive Bias Catalog](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/cognitive-bias-catalog/) — Find biases and the counter-techniques or commands that address them.

### Adversarial

- [Devil's Advocacy](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/devils-advocacy/) — Structured strongest-case against the prevailing view.
- [Premortem Analysis](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/premortem-analysis/) — Assume failure; work backward to causes and preventions.
- [Team A / Team B Analysis](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/team-a-team-b-analysis/) — Two teams argue opposing options, then swap and defend the other side.
- [BATNA](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/batna/) — Best Alternative to a Negotiated Agreement — know your walk-away before negotiating.
- [Divergence–Convergence](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/divergence-convergence/) — Explicit separate phases for generating vs. narrowing options.
- [Inversion](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/invert/) — Ask what would guarantee failure, then turn the answers into avoidance rules.
- [Steelman](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/steelman/) — Build the strongest version of each side before debate or devil's advocacy.
- [Misuse & Abuse Cases](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/misuse/) — Examine how an artifact could be misused, gamed, or harm vulnerable users.

### Strategic

- [Alternative Futures Analysis](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/alternative-futures-analysis/) — Multiple plausible futures against which to stress-test the plan.
- [Indicators / Signposts of Change](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/indicators-signposts-of-change/) — Early warnings that a given future is emerging.
- [High Impact / Low Probability Analysis](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/high-impact-low-probability-analysis/) — Tail risks — unlikely but catastrophic scenarios.
- [SWOT Analysis](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/swot-analysis/) — Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats for an option or entity.
- [Stakeholder Mapping](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/stakeholder-mapping/) — Who decides, who influences, who blocks, who suffers consequences.
- [Shifting the Burden](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/shifting-the-burden/) — Push past the obvious fix to ask who or what structurally bears the load.
- [Launch Readiness](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/launch/) — Check product go-live readiness across operations, communications, metrics, rollback, and misuse.
- [Reversibility](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/reversibility/) — Classify one-way and two-way doors, then match the decision process to the cost of reversal.

### Extension

- [Reference Class Forecasting](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/reference-class-forecasting/) — Estimate from the distribution of outcomes in similar past cases.
- [Pre-parade](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/pre-parade/) — Imagine surprising success; surface hidden upside dependencies.
- [Cynefin Domain Check](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/cynefin-domain-check/) — Classify context as clear, complicated, complex, or chaotic.
- [Polarity Mapping](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/polarity-mapping/) — Manage ongoing tensions (poles) instead of false either/or solutions.
- [Discovery-Driven Planning](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/discovery-driven-planning/) — Learning milestones to test assumptions before scaling.
- [Kill Criteria](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/kill-criteria/) — Explicit tripwires that trigger stop, pivot, or rollback.
- [Second-Order Effects](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/second-order-effects/) — Consequences of consequences; systemic feedback.
- [Narrative vs Evidence Audit](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/narrative-vs-evidence-audit/) — Where compelling story outruns disconfirmable data.
- [Decision Journal](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/decision-journal/) — Personal log of decisions and confidence for later calibration.

### Cultural

- [Cultural Perception Framework](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/cultural-perception-framework/) — How different cultural actors perceive the same events.
- [Onion Model](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/onion-model/) — Peel cultural layers from artifacts to deep assumptions.
- [6 Empathetic Questions](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/6-empathetic-questions/) — Questions that build understanding of another's frame without judgment.
- [Deception Detection](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/deception-detection/) — MOM-POP-EVE-MOSES checklist + ACH when active deception is plausible.
- [TRIZ](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/triz/) — Systematic inventive problem-solving from contradictions (Altshuller).
- [Culture / Worldview Mapping](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/culture/) — Map what stakeholders see, value, fear, and want to expose worldview collisions.

### Communication

- [Storytelling](/RedTeam/handbook/techniques/storytelling/) — Narrative form to communicate findings and alternatives persuasively.
- [Decision Record](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/record/) — Capture the decision, assumptions, dissent, tripwires, and review date in an ADR-style artifact.
