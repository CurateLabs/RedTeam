Group dynamics, groupthink symptoms, and the red team role in decision support.

## Concepts & tools in this chapter

- [Group Dynamics](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/group-dynamics/) — How groups form norms, status hierarchies, and cohesion — the substrate groupthink grows on.
- [Groupthink](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/groupthink/) — Collective pressure for conformity that suppresses dissent and produces overconfident, under-examined decisions (Irving Janis).
- [Confirmation Bias](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/confirmation-bias/) — Seeking and overweighting evidence that confirms what we already believe.
- [Decision Support](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/decision-support/) — Red Team role: improve decision quality, not own the outcome — inform, don't replace, the decider.
