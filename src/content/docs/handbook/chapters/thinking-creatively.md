The creative thought process as a sequence: problem-finding through communication — not a single brainstorm.

## Concepts & tools in this chapter

- [Problem-Finding](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/problem-finding/) — Decide whether you're solving the right problem — reframe before generating solutions.
- [Preparation](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/preparation/) — Gather information, constraints, and diverse inputs before ideation.
- [Ideation](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/ideation/) — Generate volume and variety of options without premature judgment.
- [Idea Verification](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/idea-verification/) — Test ideas against evidence, feasibility, and assumptions — distinct from killing ideas during ideation.
- [Communication](/RedTeam/handbook/concepts/communication/) — Package the outcome so decision-makers can act — clarity and narrative matter.

## RedTeam commands

- `/redteam ideate`
- `/redteam frame`
- `/redteam converge`
- `/redteam critique`

## RedTeam note

RedTeam separates `ideate` and `converge` explicitly.
