# Chapter 6 — Thinking Creatively

The creative thought process as a sequence: problem-finding through communication — not a single brainstorm.

## Concepts & tools in this chapter

- [Problem-Finding](../concepts/problem-finding.md) — Decide whether you're solving the right problem — reframe before generating solutions.
- [Preparation](../concepts/preparation.md) — Gather information, constraints, and diverse inputs before ideation.
- [Ideation](../concepts/ideation.md) — Generate volume and variety of options without premature judgment.
- [Idea Verification](../concepts/idea-verification.md) — Test ideas against evidence, feasibility, and assumptions — distinct from killing ideas during ideation.
- [Communication](../concepts/communication.md) — Package the outcome so decision-makers can act — clarity and narrative matter.
