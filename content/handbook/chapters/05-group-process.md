# Chapter 5 — Group Process & Decision Support

Group dynamics, groupthink symptoms, and the red team role in decision support.

## Concepts & tools in this chapter

- [Group Dynamics](../concepts/group-dynamics.md) — How groups form norms, status hierarchies, and cohesion — the substrate groupthink grows on.
- [Groupthink](../concepts/groupthink.md) — Collective pressure for conformity that suppresses dissent and produces overconfident, under-examined decisions (Irving Janis).
- [Confirmation Bias](../concepts/confirmation-bias.md) — Seeking and overweighting evidence that confirms what we already believe.
- [Decision Support](../concepts/decision-support.md) — Red Team role: improve decision quality, not own the outcome — inform, don't replace, the decider.
