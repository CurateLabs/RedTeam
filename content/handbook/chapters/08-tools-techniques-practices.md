# Chapter 8 — Tools, Techniques & Practices

The technique catalog for Handbook v10. Most tools support **ACT**, **GTM**, or **both**. Tools are meant to **sequence**, not stand alone.

Browse by category below. Each item has its own page in [`ttps/`](../ttps/) or [`concepts/`](../concepts/) for v10 commands.


### Facilitation

- [ACT–GTM Matrix](../ttps/act-gtm-matrix.md) — Table matching tools to ACT, GTM, or both — pick and sequence by situation.
- [Ideal Group Process](../ttps/ideal-group-process.md) — Diverge → analyze → debate → converge loop with rotating ACT/GTM tools.
- [1-2-4-Whole Group](../ttps/1-2-4-whole-group.md) — Individual → pairs → fours → whole group; staged inclusion of every voice.
- [1 on 1, 2 on 2, Exchange Emissaries](../ttps/1-on-1-2-on-2-exchange-emissaries.md) — Subgroup dialog with emissaries cross-pollinating between groups.
- [Circle of Voices](../ttps/circle-of-voices.md) — Round-robin: each speaks once before anyone speaks twice.
- [Circular Response](../ttps/circular-response.md) — Structured response pattern ensuring respectful listening and ordered contribution.
- [Fishbowl](../ttps/fishbowl.md) — Inner group discusses, outer observes; swap roles to surface dynamics.
- [Think-Write-Share](../ttps/think-write-share.md) — Silent individual write before any discussion — defeats anchoring.
- [Troika Consulting](../ttps/troika-consulting.md) — Client presents; three consultants discuss while client listens.
- [Gallery Walk](../ttps/gallery-walk.md) — Post ideas; participants silently annotate and build on others' work.
- [Dot Voting](../ttps/dot-voting.md) — Allocate limited votes across options for quick prioritization.

### Divergent

- [Brainstorming](../ttps/brainstorming.md) — Volume-first idea generation with deferred judgment.
- [5 Will Get You 25](../ttps/5-will-get-you-25.md) — Each person generates five ideas; combine for quantity before quality.
- [Yes, And…](../ttps/yes-and.md) — Build on prior ideas without blocking — improv rule for ideation phases.
- [Outside-In Thinking](../ttps/outside-in-thinking.md) — Import solutions or frames from unrelated domains.
- [Mind Mapping](../ttps/mind-mapping.md) — Branching visual map from a central concept.
- [String of Pearls](../ttps/string-of-pearls.md) — Sequential "what if" chain along a narrative thread.
- [What If? Analysis](../ttps/what-if-analysis.md) — Explore consequences when a key variable changes.
- [My 15%](../ttps/my-15-percent.md) — Each person owns the slice of the problem they can influence.
- [Appreciative Interview](../ttps/appreciative-interview.md) — Explore what works before fixing what doesn't — expands solution space.

### Analytical

- [5 Whys](../ttps/5-whys.md) — Iterative why-questions to reach root causes and hidden assumptions.
- [Analysis of Competing Hypotheses (ACH)](../ttps/analysis-of-competing-hypotheses.md) — Matrix scoring evidence against mutually exclusive hypotheses.
- [Key Assumptions Check](../ttps/key-assumptions-check.md) — List what must be true for the plan to work; flag critical unvalidated beliefs.
- [Assumption Sensitivity Analysis](../ttps/assumption-sensitivity-analysis.md) — Rank assumptions by what happens if each is wrong (collapse vs. absorb).
- [Argument Deconstruction](../ttps/argument-deconstruction.md) — Separate premises, inference, and conclusion; test each link.
- [Frame Audit](../ttps/frame-audit.md) — Examine the lens defining the problem — scope, metrics, causality, values.
- [4 Ways of Seeing](../ttps/4-ways-of-seeing.md) — Reframe as problem, polarity, pattern, or opportunity.
- [Problem Restatement](../ttps/problem-restatement.md) — Rephrase the problem multiple ways before solving.
- [6 Words](../ttps/6-words.md) — Distill the issue to six words — forces clarity.
- [SEE-I](../ttps/see-i.md) — State, Elaborate, Exemplify, Illustrate — clarify before debate.
- [Analogy Suitability Analysis](../ttps/analogy-suitability-analysis.md) — Test whether the analogy in use actually fits.
- [Logic of Failure](../ttps/logic-of-failure.md) — How can well-intentioned system logic still produce failure?
- [Critical Variables (CVs)](../ttps/critical-variables.md) — Dynamic factors that drive outcomes — often mapped to an Operating Landscape Map.
- [Who Else?](../ttps/who-else.md) — Whose perspective is missing from this analysis?
- [Who Am I?](../concepts/who-am-i.md) — Introspective identity/bias surfacing before engaging others.

### Adversarial

- [Devil's Advocacy](../ttps/devils-advocacy.md) — Structured strongest-case against the prevailing view.
- [Premortem Analysis](../ttps/premortem-analysis.md) — Assume failure; work backward to causes and preventions.
- [Team A / Team B Analysis](../ttps/team-a-team-b-analysis.md) — Two teams argue opposing options, then swap and defend the other side.
- [BATNA](../ttps/batna.md) — Best Alternative to a Negotiated Agreement — know your walk-away before negotiating.
- [Divergence–Convergence](../ttps/divergence-convergence.md) — Explicit separate phases for generating vs. narrowing options.

### Strategic

- [Alternative Futures Analysis](../ttps/alternative-futures-analysis.md) — Multiple plausible futures against which to stress-test the plan.
- [Indicators / Signposts of Change](../ttps/indicators-signposts-of-change.md) — Early warnings that a given future is emerging.
- [High Impact / Low Probability Analysis](../ttps/high-impact-low-probability-analysis.md) — Tail risks — unlikely but catastrophic scenarios.
- [SWOT Analysis](../ttps/swot-analysis.md) — Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats for an option or entity.
- [Stakeholder Mapping](../ttps/stakeholder-mapping.md) — Who decides, who influences, who blocks, who suffers consequences.
- [Shifting the Burden](../ttps/shifting-the-burden.md) — Push past the obvious fix to ask who or what structurally bears the load.

### Extension

- [Reference Class Forecasting](../ttps/reference-class-forecasting.md) — Estimate from the distribution of outcomes in similar past cases.
- [Pre-parade](../ttps/pre-parade.md) — Imagine surprising success; surface hidden upside dependencies.
- [Cynefin Domain Check](../ttps/cynefin-domain-check.md) — Classify context as clear, complicated, complex, or chaotic.
- [Polarity Mapping](../ttps/polarity-mapping.md) — Manage ongoing tensions (poles) instead of false either/or solutions.
- [Discovery-Driven Planning](../ttps/discovery-driven-planning.md) — Learning milestones to test assumptions before scaling.
- [Kill Criteria](../ttps/kill-criteria.md) — Explicit tripwires that trigger stop, pivot, or rollback.
- [Second-Order Effects](../ttps/second-order-effects.md) — Consequences of consequences; systemic feedback.
- [Narrative vs Evidence Audit](../ttps/narrative-vs-evidence-audit.md) — Where compelling story outruns disconfirmable data.
- [Decision Journal](../ttps/decision-journal.md) — Personal log of decisions and confidence for later calibration.

### Cultural

- [Cultural Perception Framework](../ttps/cultural-perception-framework.md) — How different cultural actors perceive the same events.
- [Onion Model](../ttps/onion-model.md) — Peel cultural layers from artifacts to deep assumptions.
- [6 Empathetic Questions](../ttps/6-empathetic-questions.md) — Questions that build understanding of another's frame without judgment.
- [Deception Detection](../ttps/deception-detection.md) — MOM-POP-EVE-MOSES checklist + ACH when active deception is plausible.
- [TRIZ](../ttps/triz.md) — Systematic inventive problem-solving from contradictions (Altshuller).

### Communication

- [Storytelling](../ttps/storytelling.md) — Narrative form to communicate findings and alternatives persuasively.

### v10 commands {#v10-commands}

- [Outside View / Reference Class Forecasting](../concepts/outside-view.md) — Base-rate thinking: what happened in the reference class of similar past attempts?
- [Inversion](../concepts/invert.md) — Munger-style: what would guarantee failure? Flip into avoidance rules.
- [Incentive & Goodhart Analysis](../concepts/incentives.md) — How metrics and incentives get gamed; perverse second-order effects.
- [Ladder of Inference](../concepts/ladder.md) — Argyris: separate observable data from interpretation and conclusion.
- [Steelman](../concepts/steelman.md) — Strongest version of each side before debate or devil's advocacy.
- [Calibration](../concepts/calibrate.md) — Grade claims as fact/inference/guess; attach confidence and disconfirmers.
- [Sequence / Playbooks](../concepts/sequence.md) — Chained `/redteam` commands for decision types with time budgets.
- [Culture / Worldview Mapping](../concepts/culture.md) — Stakeholder see/value/fear/want map — cultural empathy in practice for teams and products.
- [Launch Readiness](../concepts/launch.md) — Product go-live checklist: ops, comms, metrics, rollback, misuse.
- [RFC Review](../concepts/rfc.md) — Engineering decision doc review — options, reversibility, operations.
- [Misuse & Abuse Cases](../concepts/misuse.md) — How artifact could be misused, gamed, or harm vulnerable users.
- [Reversibility](../concepts/reversibility.md) — One-way vs two-way doors (Bezos); match process to reversibility.
- [Decision Record](../concepts/record.md) — ADR-style artifact with assumptions, dissent, tripwires, review date.
- [Cognitive Bias Catalog](../concepts/cognitive-bias-catalog.md) — Index of biases mapped to counter-techniques and commands.
