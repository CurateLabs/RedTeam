# Hofstede's Country Profiles

**Chapter:** [Chapter 3 — Cultural Empathy](../chapters/03-cultural-empathy.md)

## Summary

Power distance, uncertainty avoidance, individualism, masculinity/femininity, long-term orientation indices.

## When to use

International rollout, global teams — as starting hypothesis only.

## Method

Use indices to generate questions, not stereotypes. Validate with local stakeholders.

## RedTeam notes

We treat Hofstede as one input; organizational culture often dominates national averages.
