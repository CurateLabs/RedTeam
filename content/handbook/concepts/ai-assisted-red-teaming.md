# AI-Assisted Red Teaming

**Chapter:** [Chapter 7 — Red Teaming with AI & Humans in the Loop](../chapters/07-ai-and-humans-in-the-loop.md)
**ACT/GTM:** Both
**Command:** `/redteam challenge`

## Summary

Use AI as loyal opposition — challenge plans and surface risks — while the human owns stakes, judgment, and commitment.

## When to use

Any `/redteam` session in an AI harness; async document review; copilot decision support.

## Method

Human frames decision and provides source material. AI runs structured techniques. Human verifies, decides, and records.
