# Divergence–Convergence

**Chapter:** [Chapter 8 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/08-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** Both
**Command:** `/redteam ideate`

## Summary

Explicit separate phases for generating vs. narrowing options.

## When to use

60+ min; teams mix critique into brainstorming.

## Method

Phase 1: ideate tools only. Hard gate. Phase 2: converge tools only.

## RedTeam notes

Category: **adversarial**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
