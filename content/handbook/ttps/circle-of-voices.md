# Circle of Voices

**Chapter:** [Chapter 8 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/08-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** GTM
**Command:** `/redteam groupthink`

## Summary

Round-robin: each speaks once before anyone speaks twice.

## When to use

15–30 min; dominant voices monopolize discussion.

## Method

Facilitator calls order; no response until all have spoken one round.

## RedTeam notes

Category: **facilitation**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
