# Who Else?

**Chapter:** [Chapter 8 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/08-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** ACT
**Command:** `/redteam challenge`

## Summary

Whose perspective is missing from this analysis?

## When to use

15 min; homogenous analysis team.

## Method

List stakeholders and non-stakeholders with skin in game; add at least one missing voice.

Category: **analytical**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
