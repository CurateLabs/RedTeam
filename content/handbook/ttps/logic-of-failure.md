# Logic of Failure

**Chapter:** [Chapter 8 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/08-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** ACT
**Command:** `/redteam challenge`

## Summary

How can well-intentioned system logic still produce failure?

## When to use

Complex systems; perverse incentives; policy surprises.

## Method

Model feedback loops; ask how rational local choices produce global failure.

## RedTeam notes

Category: **analytical**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
