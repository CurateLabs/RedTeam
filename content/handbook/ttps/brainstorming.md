# Brainstorming

**Chapter:** [Chapter 8 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/08-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** GTM
**Command:** `/redteam ideate`

## Summary

Volume-first idea generation with deferred judgment.

## When to use

20–40 min familiar problems needing many options.

## Method

Quantity goal; no criticism during generation; capture all; cluster later.

## RedTeam notes

Category: **divergent**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
