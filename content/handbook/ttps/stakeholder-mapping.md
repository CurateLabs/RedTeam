# Stakeholder Mapping

**Chapter:** [Chapter 8 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/08-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** Both
**Command:** `/redteam review`

## Summary

Who decides, who influences, who blocks, who suffers consequences.

## When to use

30 min; political risk; rollout planning.

## Method

Map power vs interest; identify blockers and champions; plan engagement.
