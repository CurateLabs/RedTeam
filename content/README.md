Applied critical thinking skills for AI agents — **29 commands**, a full handbook, and a `.redteam/` workspace for decision reviews.

Red teaming here means **structured challenge of plans and decisions**, not cybersecurity penetration testing.

## Quick start

```bash
npx redteam install
```

Reload your harness, then in chat:

```
/redteam challenge our Q3 product strategy
/redteam sequence product launch for payments v2
```

No install? Paste a plan and invoke any `/redteam` command — chat-only mode works out of the box.

## What you get

- **Loyal opposition** — challenge thinking to improve decisions, not to win arguments
- **Structured techniques** — premortems, ACH, devil's advocacy, outside-view, frame audits, and more
- **AI-native** — Chapter 7 covers humans-in-the-loop and AI output quality (`/redteam ai-check`)
- **Living handbook** — Handbook v10 extends the defunct UFMCS v9 lineage for civilian and product use
