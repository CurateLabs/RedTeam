Handbook v10 acknowledges v9.0 as its predecessor. We carry forward the intent — applied critical thinking, cultural empathy, groupthink mitigation, and structured techniques — without presenting RedTeam output as Army doctrine.

## What v10 kept from v9

- **Loyal opposition** — challenge thinking to improve decisions, not to win arguments
- **ACT / GTM** — technique labels for critical thinking and group process
- Structured techniques: premortems, ACH, assumption checks, frame audits, facilitation patterns
- **Cultural empathy** — cross-cultural communication, perception mapping, empathetic inquiry
- Emphasis on dissent, alternatives, and making implicit assumptions explicit
