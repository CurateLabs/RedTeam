# Mind Mapping

**Chapter:** [Chapter 7 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/07-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** ACT
**Command:** `/redteam ideate`

## Summary

Branching visual map from a central concept.

## When to use

20–40 min; explore branches of a complex issue.

## Method

Central node → branches → sub-branches; use colors for themes; then prioritize branches.

## RedTeam modifications

Category: **divergent**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
