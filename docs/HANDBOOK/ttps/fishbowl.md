# Fishbowl

**Chapter:** [Chapter 7 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/07-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** GTM
**Command:** `/redteam groupthink`

## Summary

Inner group discusses, outer observes; swap roles to surface dynamics.

## When to use

30–60 min; large group; need observation of group dynamics.

## Method

Inner circle discusses while outer listens; swap; debrief what outer observed.

## RedTeam notes

Category: **facilitation**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
