# Team A / Team B Analysis

**Chapter:** [Chapter 7 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/07-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** Both
**Command:** `/redteam devils-advocate`

## Summary

Two teams argue opposing options, then swap and defend the other side.

## When to use

45–60 min; two viable options; empathy for opposition lacking.

## Method

Assign teams → argue → swap → defend other side → plenary compare.

## RedTeam modifications

Category: **adversarial**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
