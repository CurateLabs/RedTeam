# 5 Will Get You 25

**Chapter:** [Chapter 7 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/07-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** GTM
**Command:** `/redteam ideate`

## Summary

Each person generates five ideas; combine for quantity before quality.

## When to use

20 min; group stuck at 2–3 obvious options.

## Method

5 ideas per person silently → share → merge duplicates → target 25+ raw ideas.

## RedTeam notes

Category: **divergent**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
