**Chapter:** [Chapter 7 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/07-tools-techniques-practices.md)

**Command:** `/redteam futures`

## Summary

Tail risks — unlikely but catastrophic scenarios.

## When to use

30 min; risk registers ignore low-probability catastrophes.

## Method

List HILP events → assess impact → decide hedge vs accept vs monitor.
