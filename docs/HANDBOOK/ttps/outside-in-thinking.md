# Outside-In Thinking

**Chapter:** [Chapter 7 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/07-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** ACT
**Command:** `/redteam ideate`

## Summary

Import solutions or frames from unrelated domains.

## When to use

Domain trapped; incremental options only.

## Method

Ask: who solved a analogous problem elsewhere? What would [unlikely industry] do?

## RedTeam modifications

Category: **divergent**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
