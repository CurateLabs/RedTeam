# Critical Variables (CVs)

**Chapter:** [Chapter 7 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/07-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** ACT
**Command:** `/redteam assumptions`

## Summary

Dynamic factors that drive outcomes — often mapped to an Operating Landscape Map.

## When to use

Operating environment framing; planning in volatile contexts.

## Method

Identify CVs → map interactions across domains → prioritize for monitoring and planning.

## RedTeam modifications

Category: **analytical**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
