# Gallery Walk

**Chapter:** [Chapter 7 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/07-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** GTM
**Command:** `/redteam ideate`

## Summary

Post ideas; participants silently annotate and build on others' work.

## When to use

20–40 min; many ideas; introverts prefer writing.

## Method

Post ideas on walls/docs; silent review and stickers/comments; then discuss themes.

## RedTeam modifications

Category: **facilitation**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
