# 6 Empathetic Questions

**Chapter:** [Chapter 7 — Tools, Techniques & Practices](../chapters/07-tools-techniques-practices.md)
**ACT/GTM:** Both
**Command:** `/redteam challenge`

## Summary

Questions that build understanding of another's frame without judgment.

## When to use

15 min; before judging another stakeholder irrational.

## Method

Use open questions focused on their goals, fears, and constraints — not cross-examination.

## RedTeam notes

Category: **cultural**. RedTeam may extend or alias this technique.
