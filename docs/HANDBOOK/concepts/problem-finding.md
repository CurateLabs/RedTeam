# Problem-Finding

**Chapter:** [Chapter 6 — Thinking Creatively](../chapters/06-thinking-creatively.md)
**Command:** `/redteam frame`

## Summary

Decide whether you're solving the right problem — reframe before generating solutions.

## When to use

Symptom-fixing, recurring failures, stakeholder disagreement on the ask.

## Method

Problem restatement, frame audit, 4 ways of seeing, 6 words.
