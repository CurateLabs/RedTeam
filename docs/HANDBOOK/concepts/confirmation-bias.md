# Confirmation Bias

**Chapter:** [Chapter 5 — Group Process & Decision Support](../chapters/05-group-process.md)
**ACT/GTM:** ACT
**Command:** `/redteam ach`

## Summary

Seeking and overweighting evidence that confirms what we already believe.

## When to use

Intelligence analysis, post-hoc justification of a favored option.

## Method

ACH, devil's advocacy, seek disconfirming evidence explicitly.
