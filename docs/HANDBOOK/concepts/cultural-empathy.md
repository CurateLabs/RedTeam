# Cultural Empathy

**Chapter:** [Chapter 1 — Foundational Concepts](../chapters/01-foundational-concepts.md)
**ACT/GTM:** Both
**Command:** `/redteam culture`

## Summary

Understand how cultural frames shape what others see, value, and fear — across nations, organizations, teams, and disciplines. Practice empathetic cross-cultural communication; counter ethnocentrism and projection.

## When to use

Cross-cultural planning, multi-stakeholder decisions, org silos, or when "they just don't get it" appears.

## Method

Map actor worldviews and communication patterns. Use cultural frameworks as hypotheses, not stereotypes. Ask empathetic questions before judging intent.

## RedTeam notes

Civilian adaptation of source-handbook techniques. We update and extend over time — not verbatim doctrine.
