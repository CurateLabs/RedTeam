# Applied Critical Thinking (ACT)

**Chapter:** [Chapter 1 — Foundational Concepts](../chapters/01-foundational-concepts.md)
**ACT/GTM:** ACT
**Command:** `/redteam challenge`

## Summary

Deliberately analyze how we perceive, interpret, and reason — make implicit assumptions explicit and test them.

## When to use

Whenever reasoning quality matters and autopilot, bias, or time pressure may degrade decisions.

## Method

Slow down, ask why, deconstruct arguments, challenge assumptions, generate alternatives. Scale tool depth to time available.
