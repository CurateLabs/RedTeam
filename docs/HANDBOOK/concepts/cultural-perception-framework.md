# Cultural Perception Framework

**Chapter:** [Chapter 3 — Cultural Empathy](../chapters/03-cultural-empathy.md)
**ACT/GTM:** ACT
**Command:** `/redteam frame`

## Summary

Structured method to surface how different actors perceive the same situation through their cultural lens.

## When to use

Multi-stakeholder conflict where each side has a coherent but incompatible narrative.

## Method

For each actor: what do they see, value, fear, and want? Compare perception maps.

## RedTeam notes

Civilian adaptation of source-handbook techniques. We update and extend over time — not verbatim doctrine.
