# Ideation

**Chapter:** [Chapter 6 — Thinking Creatively](../chapters/06-thinking-creatively.md)
**ACT/GTM:** GTM
**Command:** `/redteam ideate`

## Summary

Generate volume and variety of options without premature judgment.

## When to use

After problem is framed; before any voting or ranking.

## Method

Brainstorming, gallery walk, yes-and, outside-in — defer evaluation.
