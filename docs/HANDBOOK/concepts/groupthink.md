# Groupthink

**Chapter:** [Chapter 5 — Group Process & Decision Support](../chapters/05-group-process.md)
**ACT/GTM:** GTM

## Summary

Collective pressure for conformity that suppresses dissent and produces overconfident, under-examined decisions (Irving Janis).

## When to use

Fast consensus, homogeneous team, stress, external threat narrative, moral certainty.

## Method

Screen for symptoms: illusion of unanimity, self-censorship, mindguards, invulnerability, rationalization.

## RedTeam notes

Civilian adaptation of source-handbook techniques. We update and extend over time — not verbatim doctrine.
