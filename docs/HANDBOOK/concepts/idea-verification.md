# Idea Verification

**Chapter:** [Chapter 6 — Thinking Creatively](../chapters/06-thinking-creatively.md)
**Command:** `/redteam critique`

## Summary

Test ideas against evidence, feasibility, and assumptions — distinct from killing ideas during ideation.

## When to use

After divergent phase closes.

## Method

Assumptions check, ACH, small experiments, critique scoring.

## RedTeam notes

Civilian adaptation of source-handbook techniques. We update and extend over time — not verbatim doctrine.
