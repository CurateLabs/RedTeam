# Self-Awareness & Reflection

**Chapter:** [Chapter 1 — Foundational Concepts](../chapters/01-foundational-concepts.md)

## Summary

Know your own biases, temperament, and communication patterns before trying to challenge others.

## When to use

Before facilitating, before devil's advocacy, when strong personal attachment to an outcome exists.

## Method

Introspection, journaling, Who Am I? exercise, name your assumptions and temperament limits upfront.
